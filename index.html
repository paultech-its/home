Fixmigo

www.fixmigo.com

Fixmigo is a Prepaid IT Support platform that utilizes an automated but simplified IT Service Management (ITSM) system. The platform connects global customers to highly qualified Independent IT Geeks & Professionals (called techmigo). Fixmigo was initiated out of passion in IT & compassion to hopefully resolve 3 Major Areas of Concern that the recent global crisis has caused - Financially struggling companies with IT expenses, the massive increase in unemployment, and non-techie consumers forced to learn IT.

Kumpramart

www.kumpramart.com

Kumpramart PH is a B2B (Business-to-Business) E-Commerce platform that connects Filipino online resellers or small-medium businesses to legit suppliers. The platform's goals are for Filipinos to earn bigger than less, remove scammers & low-quality products, & provide sellable products.
