Kumpramart is a B2B-inspired (Business-to-Business) e-commerce platform that is open to the consumer public. The website allows anyone (consumers or businesses) to purchase varieties of whole-sale quality products directly from the manufacturers.
Kumpramart's goal is to help make it easier for anyone who wishes to start a small or medium-sized business by getting a direct manufacturer's price. Thus, Kumpramart does not add any interest or retail prices & only gets a small commission for every online transaction.
